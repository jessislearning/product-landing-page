One Bedroom Unit at The Residences at Commonwealth

Our one-bedroom unit at The Residences at Commonwealth is the perfect staycation spot for anyone looking for a green escape from the hustle and bustle of Metro Manila.

The Residences at Commonwealth is a 4.4-hectare gated community, featuring green spaces nestled within several mid-rise buildings. Just outside the property are several restaurants, a mall (with a bowling alley and billiards), bars, spas, banks, and other businesses.

Your stay includes:

  • One bedroom, one private bathroom, living room and dining area
  • Full kitchen - rice cooker, induction stove, microwave, rice cooker, refrigerator, coffee press, dinnerware, utensils, cookware
  • Fiber Internet
  • Semi-double bed
  • Smart TV (with Netflix)
  • Towels, shampoo, body wash, hand soap
  • Hot shower and bidet
  • Indoor washing machine
  • One parking slot (please indicate your request)

Amenities

The Residences at Commonwealth offers wide green open spaces, and exclusive amenities. You can take it slow, or you can choose to be active. There's a little bit of something for everyone!

Inside the property, you can find:

  • The Clubhouse (Arts and Crafts Studio, Kindergarten Playroom, Gym, Rock and Roll Studio, Screening Room, Show Kitchen)
  • The Great Lawn
  • Basketball Court
  • Swimming Pool (Lap pool, Main pool, Kiddie Pool)
  • Playground
  • Convenience Stores, Laundry & Water Services, Hair Salon

The Residences at Commonwealth is pet-friendly, with plenty of open spaces to walk and enjoy.
